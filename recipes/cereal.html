Anabolic Cereal with Honey and Protein

Energizing cereal with some honey, corn flakes, fruits and protein source of choice

An anabolic post-workout meal to fuel both your energy receptors after a lengthy workout

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Ingredient list

  • 30 grams of corn flakes
  • 10 ml of honey
  • One medium banana
  • Depending on the desired consistency use:
    • 100 grams of Greek yoghurt
    • 200 ml of Milk with 25 grams of Protein Powder
