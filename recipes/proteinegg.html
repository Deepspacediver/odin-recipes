Egg Omlette filled with (not only) Protein Powder

An Egg Omelette bombed with 40 grams of protein and sweet fruits of your choice

A delicous way to spice up your mundane eggs with a little sweetness, some fibre, and tons of muscle building protein

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Ingredient list

  • 3 eggs
  • 20 grams of protein powder
  • 125 grams of zero fat greek yogurt or skyr yogurt
  • 1/2 tea spoon of baking powder
  • Cinammon powder
  • Fruit of choice, preferrably soft ones (bananas, berriess etc.)
  • *Artifical sweetener

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix 10 grams of protein powder with 75 grams of yogurt of choice
  2. Creack 3 eggs into the mix and stir it up until everything is well mixed together
  3. Mix in the cinammon and baking powder
  4. Pour the mixture onto a hot pan on a low heat
  5. While you wait mix the rest of the protein powder and yogurt together>
  6. Cut up your fruits
  7. Check up on your omelette from time to time and when it's time, flip it to the other side
  8. Once the omelette is done take it off the pan
  9. Coat it with the yogurt-protein cream and add your fruits
